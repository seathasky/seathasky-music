If you are interested in an indie music composer for your next game or media project, I am your guy!

My name is Matthew Centonze, also know as "Seathasky".
I have 23+ years experience in the music industry as a freelance music composer and electronic music artist.
Whether you're looking for game, orchestral music, horror, sci-fi, electronic, ambient, sad piano or anything you can think of - I can help you. Take a minute to have a listen to some hand selected examples in my music sidereels below. I can be pretty versatile but also have my own unique flair!

Contact me If you want to discuss music for your next project!
